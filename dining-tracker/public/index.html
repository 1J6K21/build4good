    Mindful Eating

    MACRO GUIDELINES

    PROTEIN FOCUS

    "Aim for 0.8g to 1g of protein per pound of body weight to support muscle recovery and growth."

    HEALTHY HABIT

    Hydration First

    Did you know drinking a glass of water before meals can help with portion control and metabolic function? Stay hydrated!

    CORE PRINCIPLES
    • CONSISTENCY: Small, daily improvements lead to sustainable long-term results.
    • BALANCE: Focus on whole foods while allowing for flexibility in your diet.
    • SLEEP: Quality rest is the foundation of a healthy metabolism.
    FIBER INTAKE

    The Fiber Factor

    Fiber keeps you full longer and supports digestive health. Look for whole grains, beans, and colorful vegetables on your plate.

    QUICK TIPS
    • Prioritize Protein.
    • Add greens to every meal.
    • Track, don't obsess.
    • Listen to your hunger cues.
    METABOLIC HEALTH

    STRENGTH TRAINING

    Building muscle increases your resting metabolic rate, meaning you burn more calories even while at rest. Keep moving!

    SMART SNACKING

    Choose Wisely

    Opt for high-protein or high-fiber snacks to keep energy levels stable throughout the day. Nuts, seeds, and fruit are great options.

    REST & RECOVERY

    Listen to Your Body

    Rest days are just as important as training days. They allow your muscles to repair and your central nervous system to recover.

    MEAL TIMING

    Finding Your Rhythm

    The best meal timing is the one that fits your schedule and allows you to remain consistent with your goals. Pre-workout fuel is key!

    WHOLE FOODS

    Single Ingredients

    When possible, choose foods with a single ingredient. It simplifies tracking and ensures you're getting high-quality fuel.

    MINDSET MATTERS

    Progress Over Perfection

    Don't let one 'off' day derail your progress. Get back on track with your next meal and focus on the long game.
